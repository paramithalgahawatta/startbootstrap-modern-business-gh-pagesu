History Of Ayurveda

Ayurveda, originating over 5,000 years ago in India, is a holistic discipline that focuses on preventing and curing ailments through the synergy of mind and body. It has influenced traditional medicine, addressing mental, spiritual, and physical aspects of life. Ayurveda's core concept is based on the Panchamahabhuta, which consists of earth, fire, water, air, and space.

Sri lankan traditionl treatments

Sri Lankan traditional treatment refers to the indigenous healing practices and therapies that have been passed down through generations in Sri Lanka. These treatments often incorporate a blend of Ayurveda, indigenous medicine, and traditional remedies. They include herbal medicine, oil massages, steam treatments, detoxification therapies, and various forms of energy healing. Sri Lankan traditional treatment aims to restore balance and promote overall well-being by addressing the root causes of illness and imbalances in the body and mind.
